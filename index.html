Privacy Policy

View Privacy Policy

StikNES respects your privacy. We do not collect or store any personal data unless you explicitly provide it, outside of what Apple automatically collects. All emulator usage is handled locally on your device. Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and protected by Cloudflare, ensuring a secure and reliable browsing experience. The app loads announcements, error messages, and a killswitch to turn off the app from a JSON file hosted on our website.

For more details, please join our Discord.

Device Compatibility & Requirements

Supported Devices

  • iPhone SE (2nd generation) and newer
  • iPhone XS and newer
  • iPad (6th generation) and newer
  • iPad Air (3rd generation) and newer
  • iPad Pro (3rd generation) and newer
  • iPad Mini (5th generation) and newer

Operating System Requirements

StikNES requires iOS 17.0 or later.

Known Limitations

  • Older devices may experience performance issues and require more testing.
  • Emulation accuracy may vary depending on the game.
  • AirPlay support is planned for future releases but is not currently available.

Current System Support

Currently Supported: NES

Coming Soon: Flash
